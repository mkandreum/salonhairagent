## Hiring Process - CEO-LED with AI/ML Focus

### Phase 1: Role Definition & Sourcing (Week 1)
- **CEO defines role requirements** and success criteria for Founding Senior Software Engineer
- Job description focused on startup adaptability, technical excellence, and AI integration
- **Sourcing through CEO-led channels**:
  - CEO professional networks and connections
  - AI/ML technical communities and platforms
  - Direct outreach to potential candidates by CEO
  - AI-focused job boards and platforms

### Phase 2: Screening & Assessment (Week 2)
- **Initial screening**: CEO reviews all applications and selects candidates
- **Technical phone screen** (30 mins, CEO-led):
  - Assessment of technical fundamentals and problem-solving approach
  - Discussion of AI/ML experience and interest
  - Evaluation of startup adaptability and cultural fit
- **Technical challenge**: Practical coding exercise relevant to AI automation (prepared by CTO for CEO use)
- **Final interview** (60 mins, CEO-led):
  - Deep discussion on company vision and AI automation mission
  - Technical approach and architecture thinking
  - Cultural alignment and startup mindset
- **CTO involvement**: Technical consultation only upon CEO request during screening process

### Phase 3: Decision & Onboarding (Week 3-4)
- **CEO makes final hiring decision** based on comprehensive assessment
- Offer extended with competitive compensation package and significant equity stake
- **CEO-led onboarding preparation**:
  - Company mission and AI automation vision briefing
  - Introduction to existing team members and CTO
  - Access to development environments and AI tools
- **First-month agenda** (CEO oversight):
  - Week 1: Company immersion and AI architecture overview
  - Week 2: Hands-on with existing agent framework (CTO guidance)
  - Week 3: First automation project assignment
  - Week 4: Progress review and integration planning

## Success Metrics & KPIs - CEO-Led Hiring

### CEO-Led Hiring Process Metrics
- **CEO decision speed**: Time from first candidate contact to offer (target: < 4 weeks)
- **Candidate CEO satisfaction**: Feedback on CEO-led process (target: > 4.5/5.0)
- **CEO offer acceptance rate**: Offers extended vs accepted (target: > 80%)
- **CEO sourcing effectiveness**: Quality of CEO-network candidates vs external sources
- **CEO process efficiency**: Time spent vs hiring quality ratio

### Founding Engineer Success Metrics (CEO Oversight)
- **30-day (CEO evaluation)**: 
  - Completion of CEO-led onboarding program
  - First meaningful contribution to codebase
  - Integration with company mission and culture
- **90-day (CEO/CTO joint evaluation)**:
  - Delivery of first automation feature
  - Establishment of engineering practices
  - Technical contribution impact assessment
- **6-month (Transition to CTO oversight)**:
  - Significant advancement of AI automation capabilities
  - Technical leadership demonstration
  - Contribution to platform architecture
- **12-month (CTO oversight with CEO review)**:
  - Leadership in AI automation initiatives
  - Mentoring of new team members
  - Business impact measurement

### CEO Leadership & Governance Metrics
- **CEO decision quality**: New hire performance vs hiring assessment
- **CEO process adherence**: Alignment with established hiring governance
- **CEO-CTO collaboration**: Effectiveness of technical consultation process
- **CEO time investment**: Efficiency of CEO-led hiring vs results
- **Board satisfaction**: Feedback on CEO-led hiring process and outcomes

### Business Impact Metrics (CEO Accountability)
- **Platform development velocity**: Impact of founding engineer on timeline
- **AI automation coverage**: Processes automated with new capabilities
- **Technical foundation quality**: Assessment of codebase and architecture
- **Team scaling readiness**: Preparation for additional engineering hires
- **Business value delivery**: Contribution to company objectives and revenue

## Risks & Mitigation
- **Risk**: Limited pool of qualified AI/ML talent
  - **Mitigation**: Broaden search criteria, consider remote candidates, offer competitive compensation
- **Risk**: High competition for AI/ML professionals
  - **Mitigation**: Emphasize mission and impact, offer meaningful equity, highlight growth opportunities
- **Risk**: Onboarding challenges for AI-specific roles
  - **Mitigation**: Develop comprehensive AI onboarding program, assign AI mentor, provide clear success metrics
- **Risk**: Governance alignment with CEO-led process
  - **Mitigation**: Clear communication of decision authority, documented process, regular updates to board
