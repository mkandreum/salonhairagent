# Founding Senior Software Engineer - AI Process Automation

## Company Overview
We are building the future of business process automation with AI agents. Our mission is to transform how companies operate by creating intelligent, autonomous systems that understand and execute complex business processes. As a founding engineer, you'll be instrumental in building our core AI automation platform from the ground up.

## Role: Founding Senior Software Engineer
**Priority:** Critical - First Engineering Hire  
**Reporting:** Direct to CEO initially, transitioning to CTO oversight  
**Location:** Remote (Global)  
**Target Start Date:** Within 4 weeks  
**Equity:** Significant founding team equity (0.5% - 2.0%)

## The Opportunity
- Be employee #3 in a rapidly growing AI automation company
- Build the core platform that will power AI agents for business process automation
- Work directly with CEO and CTO on strategic technical decisions
- Shape engineering culture and best practices from day one
- Solve challenging problems at the intersection of AI, automation, and enterprise software

## What You'll Build
- **AI Agent Orchestration System**: Core platform for managing and coordinating AI agents
- **Process Automation Engine**: System for defining, executing, and monitoring business processes
- **LLM Integration Framework**: Abstraction layer for multiple LLM providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- **Agent Communication APIs**: RESTful and streaming APIs for agent interaction
- **Monitoring & Observability**: Real-time monitoring of AI agent performance and automation success
- **User Interfaces**: Tools for configuring, monitoring, and managing automation workflows

## Technical Requirements
### Must-Have (5+ years experience):
- **TypeScript/JavaScript** expertise with modern frameworks
- **React/Node.js** ecosystem mastery
- **API design and implementation** for distributed systems
- **System architecture** for scalable, reliable services
- **Database design** (SQL and NoSQL)
- **Testing and quality assurance** best practices

### AI/ML Experience (Strongly Preferred):
- Experience with **LangGraph, LangChain, or similar agent frameworks**
- **LLM integration** (OpenAI, Anthropic, Claude, etc.)
- **Prompt engineering** and optimization
- **Vector databases** (Pinecone, Weaviate, etc.)
- **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** systems
- Understanding of **AI agent patterns** and architectures

### Nice-to-Have:
- Experience with **business process automation** platforms
- Knowledge of **workflow orchestration** (Airflow, Temporal, etc.)
- **Cloud infrastructure** (AWS, GCP, Azure) with AI/ML services
- **DevOps and CI/CD** experience
- **Open-source contributions** or public projects
