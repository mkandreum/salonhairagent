# AI/ML Engineer (LLM Applications & Process Automation)

## About Us
We are an AI-powered process automation company dedicated to transforming business operations through intelligent agent systems. Our mission is to build AI agents that automate complex workflows, reduce manual effort, and drive operational excellence.

## Position Overview
We are seeking an AI/ML Engineer with specialization in Large Language Models (LLMs) and agent frameworks to join our founding engineering team. You will focus on developing and optimizing AI agents for business process automation, implementing intelligent features that leverage LLMs, LangGraph, and modern NLP techniques to understand and automate complex business workflows.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop and optimize AI agents for business process understanding and automation using LLMs
- Design and implement agent reasoning, decision-making, and workflow orchestration capabilities
- Implement and optimize Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems for enhanced agent knowledge
- Experiment with different LLM providers, configurations, and prompting strategies
- Evaluate and improve model performance, response quality, reliability, and safety
- Implement fine-tuning and adaptation techniques for domain-specific language understanding
- Collaborate with domain experts to understand business processes and identify automation opportunities
- Build evaluation frameworks and benchmark suites for AI agent performance assessment
- Stay current with latest research in LLMs, agent systems, and prompt engineering
- Contribute to the development of our AI agent platform and framework extensions

## Required Qualifications
- 3+ years of professional experience in AI/ML or related fields
- Strong Python programming skills (3+ years) with experience in AI/ML libraries (PyTorch, TensorFlow, Hugging Face, etc.)
- Hands-on experience with LLM frameworks (LangChain, LangGraph, LlamaIndex, AutoGen, etc.)
- Practical experience with LLM integration and API usage (OpenAI GPT, Anthropic Claude, etc.)
- Understanding of natural language processing concepts and techniques
- Experience with vector databases and embedding technologies (Pinecone, Weaviate, FAISS, etc.)
- Familiarity with prompt engineering best practices and techniques
- Knowledge of evaluation methodologies for language models and AI systems
- Proficiency with Git and collaborative development practices
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

## Preferred Qualifications
- Experience with fine-tuning LLMs for specific domains or tasks
- Knowledge of PEFT (Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning) techniques (LoRA, adapters, etc.)
- Familiarity with model quantization and optimization for inference efficiency
- Understanding of multi-agent systems and agent communication protocols
- Experience with workflow automation or business process management systems
- Background in data engineering or ETL pipeline development
- Experience with A/B testing and experimentation frameworks for AI systems
- Familiarity with MLflow or similar experiment tracking tools
- Understanding of ethics and bias mitigation in AI systems
- Open-source contributions to LLM or agent frameworks
